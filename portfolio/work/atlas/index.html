Atlas

A wayfinding system for content-heavy apps.

links
figma Figma file ↗ proto Prototype ↗

Atlas is a navigation pattern for surfaces that have more depth than they have screen space. Persistent breadcrumbs, a peek nav, and a quiet command bar that lets you jump without losing your place.

The problem

Users in deep apps often know what they want but not where it lives. Tree menus and tabs scale linearly with content; perceived complexity scales much faster.

What we explored

Three navigation primitives that compose: a breadcrumb that always shows where you are, a peek panel that shows where you could go, and a command bar that lets you skip the journey when you already know the destination.

Where it landed

A small, opinionated nav system documented as a kit. Works best in apps with at least three real levels of depth — at less than that the patterns are overkill.
